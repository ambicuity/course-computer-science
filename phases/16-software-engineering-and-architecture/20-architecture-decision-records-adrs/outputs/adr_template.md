## Context

What is the issue that we're seeing that is motivating this decision or change?

Include the following details:
- Technical constraints (performance, scale, compatibility requirements)
- Business requirements (timeline, budget, compliance)
- Organizational context (team skills, existing infrastructure, vendor relationships)
- Alternatives considered (briefly list and why they were rejected)

## Decision

What is the change that we're proposing and/or doing?

Be specific. "We will use X" is a decision. "We will evaluate options" is not.

Include:
- The specific technology, approach, or pattern chosen
- Key configuration or deployment details
- Any constraints on the choice (version, edition, configuration)

## Consequences

What becomes easier or more difficult to do because of this change?

Be honest. Include all three categories:

- **Positive**: Benefits of this decision
- **Negative**: Drawbacks, limitations, risks
- **Neutral**: Side effects that are neither good nor bad but should be noted
